Predoctoral Research

I am passionate about advancing technology accessibility and sustainability through computer and electrical engineering. My research interests include hardware and computer chip design, signal processing, digital systems, nanofabrication, and smart device development for underserved communities. My current work explores computer networking, focusing on broadband equity and internet speed test contextualization. I aim to create efficient, inclusive, and impactful technologies that bridge innovation with real-world needs.
