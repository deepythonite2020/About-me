Research Interests

I am passionate about advancing technology accessibility and sustainability through computer and electrical engineering. My research interests focus on applying artificial intelligence and machine learning to the modeling, measurement, and optimization of real-world engineering systems, with particular attention to energy efficiency, affordability, and reliability. My current work applies deep learning methods to predictive maintenance and system performance evaluation in industrial engineering contexts, resulting in a preprint manuscript on deep learning based predictive maintenance. I aim to develop adaptive, efficient, and inclusive technologies that bridge advanced engineering innovation with real-world societal needs.
